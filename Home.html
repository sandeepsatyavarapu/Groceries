SIGNIFICANCE

Veg And Fruits Importance:

Vegetables and fruit contain antioxidants and phytochemicals, or plant chemicals. These biologically active substances can help to protect you from some diseases. Scientific research shows that if you regularly eat lots of fruit and vegetables, you have a lower risk of: type 2 diabetes.

Millets And Seeds Importance:

It is a good source of protein, fibre and a number of important vitamins and minerals. Seeds are loaded with vitamins like Thiamine, Riboflavin, Niacin, Pantothenic acid, Folate, Choline, vitamin B6, vitamin C and vitamin E.

Non-veg Importance:

Non-veg sustenances are high in protein content which is a basic component for smooth working and mending of the body.
